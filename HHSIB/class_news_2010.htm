Senior Class : Caps and Gowns


The Senior class attended an assembly today Sep 14th about ordering graduation announcements and caps and gowns. Each student received a letter with instructions and information about ordering online.

Herff Jones is the company that will be supplying your graduation needs. They will be in the HHS auditorium on Conference night, Sept 24th to take orders and answer your questions. I recommend that you order as early as possible.
  • Orders should be placed on Sept 24th and Sept 25th.
  • Please mark "IB" in the lower left hand corner of your order form.
 How to contact Herff Jones:
     6009 Dr. Martin Luther King Street North
     St. Petersburg, FL 33703
     727-381-7714
     fax 727-381-7713
     www.herffjonesstpete.com

Since the IB seniors leave campus in May to begin testing, orders should be placed with Herff Jones no later than November 20th to ensure delivery in time for graduation.

Mrs. Brockinton
Last update: 09-14-2009
