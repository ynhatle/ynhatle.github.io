Teacher Wish List 2008-2009 Report

We are delighted to report on the 2008-09 Teacher Wish List Program coordinated by the HHS IB Alliance.

In the fall the Wish List Committee kicked off the "Think Big" program to get the teachers thinking about resources they could use in their classroom to enhance the instructional process.

They certainly did some great thinking! As a result of this program, teachers requested items totaling over $12,000. The District funded many of the items identified through the Wish List Program and the remainder (over $3,500) the IB Alliance funded.

Funded by the HHS IB Alliance:
  • Registration fees for 2 teachers to attend the Gifted Conference
  • IB Learner Posters (one per classroom)
  • IB Math Test Banks
  • IB Exam Paper and Markschemes
  • Biozone "Skills in Biology" Workbooks
  • Psychology Books
  • Digital Microscope w/Camera
  • Free Fall and Pendulum Set
  • Student Geiger Counter
  • Radioactive Sources
  • Surge Protectors
  • Batteries
On behalf of the teachers, we thank all supporters for your generosity and support.
