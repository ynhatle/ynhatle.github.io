Turkey Break !!! Happy Thanksgiving !!!


Hillsborough County Public Schools will be closed the entire week of Thanksgiving, November 23 - 27, 2009. In keeping with tradition at Hillsborough, the IB program will once again have a "Turkey Break" in our classes. "Turkey Break" basically means, where it is academically feasible, students will not have any homework and/or major projects due the cycle after the Thanksgiving break. The spirit behind "Turkey Break" is for our students to rest from school work, enjoy their families, stay up late, sleep in, and have fun with their friends during the week of Thanksgiving.

In addition, I am encouraging our faculty and staff to also take a "Turkey Break". I certainly am! I have many things to be thankful for in my life. Near the top of my list is the privilege to work at a school with a gifted faculty, wonderful students, and supportive families.

Have a blessed Thanksgiving!
Anthony Jones
PS: Report Cards will be given to students on Thursday, November 19, 2009

Welcome to the Hillsborough High School International Baccalaureate Alliance (HHSIB) Website.

Our objectives are:
  • Information sources about our School and IB program
  • Communication links between HHS IB Staff, Parents and Students.
Our main contents are:
  • Information about IB program (IBO link, About IB, CAS Info).
  • Information about the HHSIB program (Faculty & Staff, IB Application, HHS Link)
  • Communication from HHSIB Staff ( News, Class News, College Lab, IB Office.)
  • Information about Student activities (News, Class News, IB Calendar, Sports&Clubs, Departments,, Travel Tours)
  • Information about IB Alliance activities ( IB Alliance News, Fundraising, About IB Alliance)
  • Parent Menu.: Student online directory
  • Admin Menu: for admin only.
Hillsborough High School has been an IB World School since January 1991. It offers the IB Diploma Program. Dr. William Orr is the Principal and Mr. Anthony Jones is the Assistant Principal for Magnet Curriculum.
