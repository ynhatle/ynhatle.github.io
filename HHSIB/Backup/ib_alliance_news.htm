October 2009:

THANK YOU & WELCOME

First 2 months of school are always busy. We welcome all freshmen parents to HHS IB Alliance! Class of 2011 is officially entered the “IB years” by taking the oath and receiving IB Pin during the Pinning Ceremony on Sep 22nd. Class of 2010 is preparing for College application and selection. By now, kids are settled down with school routines, studying, Clubs and Sport activities.

As usual, we have a strong participation of parent volunteers. At the IB Alliance General Meeting Oct 6th, 2009, a new Board was elected with several new chairs added for different activities.

We would like to THANK all Parent volunteers last year and WELCOME the new volunteers this year!

If any parent have time and want to participate in certain area, please feel free to contact that Chair person. We still need underclassmen parents to help as backup so that you can continue when senior’s parents “graduate”.

Student Directory Information 2009-2010 have been collected and entered into the online Directory. Parents should have the UserIds and Passwords to access the Parent Menu. If you do not have it, please email our Webmaster here. Also please check out your child information online and let us know if there is any correction needed.

September 2009:

Parents Volunteers Invitation

There are many opportunities to participate in your child’s high school !!!

The IB Alliance is supportive of the students, teachers and staff of the IB program and would appreciate your assistance of time and/or resources. This involvement could be at any level which you feel comfortable including occasional phone call assistance, committee assistance, program support, website support,and participation on the Alliance Board.

We are still in need of filling open positions!

Please contact Rita Schonwetter to learn more about our organization. We look forward to being available to answer any questions you may have.

Also, please remember to check the website regularly!!

August 2009:

Freshman Parents Meeting Invitation

On Tuesday, August 18, 2009 from 6:30-8:00pm, the HHS IB Alliance will hold a Meet and Greet-Question and Answer session at Buchanan Middle School.

School personnel, Alliance members and a few seasoned IB students will attend to offer a brief overview of the Alliance and answer any last minute questions parents may have.

We look forward to having many freshman parents joining us!

July 2009:

Congratulations to Class of 2009 !!!

92% of the Class of 2009 earned the IB Diploma, the highest rate in HHS history. The Class of 2009 consists of 130 candidates.

We are very proud of you !! Good luck as you continue your academic journey !!
