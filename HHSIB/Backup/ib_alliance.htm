The Hillsborough High School IB Alliance, Inc. is an organization IB student parents. The goals and activities of the IB Alliance are as follows:

  • To develop and implement plans for International Baccalaureate (IB) functions. This includes the IB Directory, Pinning Ceremony, Spaghetti Dinner, Bake Sale, T-shirt Sale, Senior Banquet, Senior Lock-In, Senior Send-off, and IB Teacher Welcome Back Breakfast.
  • To provide for the instructional needs of IB teachers above and beyond what the county can support.
  • To increase opportunities for parental involvement in the IB program.
  • To increase communication among and support for IB students, parents, faculty and staff.

Membership is open to all parents of Hillsborough IB students. Your involvement is crucial to our success.
