PSAT - Wednesday, Oct. 14, 2009


The PSAT will be given to all freshmen, sophomores and juniors on Wednesday, Oct. 14. There is no cost to students for this test.

The test will begin at the start of the school day and will end at approximately 11:10 am. Students should bring their calculators and #2 pencils (not mechanical pencils).

This is the only opportunity for juniors to qualify for National Merit, National Achievement and National Hispanic Scholar status. If juniors are absent and have documentation of being ill they can try to qualify in another way and should see Mrs. Holcombe for more information.

Congrats to Joe Humphrey & The Red & Black


The 2008-09 Red & Black has been named a Pacemaker finalist by the National Scholastic Press Association. The Pacemaker is the highest honor given to publications -- and being a finalist is a HUGE deal. About half the schools on the list will be named Pacemaker winners at the convention in November. Only 3 from Florida made the list, by the way.

Congratulations on a job incredibly well done! We are proud of you!

Jen Kritch, M.A.
English Department Head
Hillsborough High School
