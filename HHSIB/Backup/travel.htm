Summer 2010 Tour: Europe at Leisure

15 days of touring: Rome, Florence, Lucerne, Paris, and London!!!

An EF Tour led by:
Mr. Z. Gast, Mrs. K. Rizzo, and Mr. M. Mikulec

Our Tour is opened to Sophomores, Juniors, rising Seniors and Parents


Expected travel dates: Wednesday, June 16th to Wednesday, June 30, 2010.
Enrollment is expected to remain open into October of 2009

Remember: All students may earn as much as 30 Creative hours by completing one of two faculty approved CAS Projects directly related to the tour

Tour #749393

Travel is really the greatest of adventures and the experience by which young people learn the most. This promises to be an awesome tour as it was chosen by the Class of 2011 and unlike so many other tours that seem to move at a breakneck speed, this one takes its time and spends a few days at each stop. It allows the travelers to take in more of what these wonderful locations have to offer. Please review the itinerary below and we are sure you will agree that to see Europe at Leisure is to really see Europe. We look forward to future participant sign-ups.
