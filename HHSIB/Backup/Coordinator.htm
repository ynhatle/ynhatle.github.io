From the IB Coordinator

School Board policy states "students who withdraw from magnet programs shall return to the regular academic program in the school to which they are assigned by the school board".

Sep 1st, 2009


We have started the year off on a positive note! It has been a wonderful opening. I have been spent the early days of school reminding the students to get involved in all things Hillsborough. We have wonderful teams, clubs, organizations, you name it. The students who are plugged into an extra-curricular organization do better in IB. Involved students benefit from a larger network of support and learn better how to manage their time.

Welcome Back,
Mr. Jones

Please be reminded of the following dates:
  • Tuesday, September 22th- 2009 IB Pinning Ceremony
    - This event is for our Junior class and their families. This is a special time for them to be officially "pinned" as IB students. The event starts at 6:30pm.
  • Thursday, September 24th - Fall Conference Night
    - Starts at 3:30pm and the final conferences begin at 8:15pm. Conferences will be held in 15 minute blocks.
    - All conferences will be scheduled through the teacher. The IB office will not be scheduling conferences this year.
    - Please email your child's teacher via the school website or EDLine in order to schedule an appointment.
  • Tuesday, October 6, 2009 - Fall IB Parent Meeting
    - Starts at promptly at 6:30pm and ends promptly at 8:00PM
    - Meeting covers important information about the upcoming IB year, news from the IB Alliance, and a presentation from Ms Gondek about college applications.
    - This meeting is mandatory for seniors and at least one parent or guardian, but all IB parents should plan to attend this important meeting.


Aug 3rd, 2009


The 2009-2010 school year is almost here. Classes start at 7:35am on Tuesday, August 25th! I want to give you a few goals over the last weeks of summer. One, please make sure you are on top of all your summer assignments. Two, please make sure they are no major issues with your schedule. If there is a major issue, please contacts us in the IB office. Finally, make sure you soak up some more rays and enjoy the rest of your summer.

The following dates are also important:
  • Thursday, August 13, 2009 - Terrier Connect - 5PM
    This event is a general orientation to Hillsborough and is for freshmen only. The event starts at 5PM
  • Saturday, August 15, 2009 - IB Freshmen Day at the Northdale YMCA - 9AM - NOON
    This event is the main orientation for incoming HHS IB freshmen. Please sign up through the YMCA.
  • Tuesday, August 18, 2009 - IB Freshmen Parent Meeting - Buchanan Middle School Cafeteria - 6:30PM
    This event is the main freshmen parent orientation and an opportunity to ask questions of the IB Alliance and the IB Coordinator.
  • Thursday, August 20, 2009 - HHS Open House - 5PM
    This event is for freshmen students to come and meet their teachers and to walk the halls of HHS!
The HHS IB turns 20 this year! It is going to be a great year.

Sincerely,

Mr. Jones
